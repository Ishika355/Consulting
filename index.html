Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I consult the nutritionist?

You can submit a consultation request through the form on the website by entering your details, writing your query, and optionally uploading any reports. The nutritionist will review and accept your request before responding.

2. Can I upload my medical reports or images?

Yes, the form allows you to upload images or PDF files of your medical reports, prescriptions, or test results.

3. What file formats are supported for uploads?

You can upload images (JPG, PNG) and PDF documents. Each file should be under 10MB in size.

4. How long does it take to get a response?

You will typically receive a response within 24–48 hours after your request has been reviewed and accepted by the nutritionist.

5. Will I be notified when my request is accepted?

Yes, once your request is accepted, you will receive a confirmation and further instructions via email.

6. Is there a cost for the consultation?

Some basic queries might be free, but detailed consultations may require a subscription. You can check available plans under the Subscriptions section.

7. What subscription plans are available?

We currently offer: Basic Plan – ₹499/month: Includes 2 consultations. Pro Plan – ₹999/month: Includes 5 consultations + personalized diet plan.

8. How do I subscribe to a plan?

Visit the Subscriptions section on the website and choose your preferred plan. Subscription processing is currently manual and will be handled after initial contact.

9. Can I update or cancel my query after submitting it?

If your request hasn’t been accepted yet, you may submit a new form with updated information. Contact support if you need assistance.

10. Is my health data secure?

Yes. All data is handled confidentially and is only accessible to the nutritionist. We do not share your information with any third parties.

Can't find the answer to your question?

If you still have questions or need personalized guidance, feel free to reach out. We're here to help you with your health and nutrition journey.
